About

Dr. Kailun Yan is a postdoctoral fellow at the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University, working with Dr. Sisi Duan. His research focuses on Web3 security, particularly DApps, smart contracts, and blockchain protocols. His work has been published in WWW 2023 and CCS 2024, earning the ACM CCS 2024 Distinguished Paper Award and the Best Practical Paper in Cybersecurity Award 2025.

He received his D.Eng. from Shandong University in 2025 (advisor: Prof. Wenrui Diao) and was a visiting student at George Mason University in 2024 (advisor: Asst. Prof. Xiaokuan Zhang). He earned his M.Eng. and B.Eng. degrees from Guangxi Normal University (advisor: Assoc. Prof. Jilian Zhang) and Changzhou University, respectively.
